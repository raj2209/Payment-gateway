About us

It is an initiative started by few college student inorder to contribute to the society.

Choice Chance Change was started in 2020 when alot of people were struggling with nobel corona virus and it impacted alot of people's live. Inorder to provide help and rise to the cause a group of friend started of 3C's.

It started with a thought that Everyone has a Choice and when given a Chance anyone can Change other's life.

Initially it had just 4 people helping the underprivileged by putting there pocket money for the weekend drives, but now there are 100+ people in the 3C's community.

The member of 3C's join in the weekend drive which are conducted on following locations:

  • Howrah
  • Gashbagan
  • kolkata
  • Liluah
